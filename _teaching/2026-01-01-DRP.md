Topics covered: Singular homology, Eilenberg-Steenrod axioms for homology, introduction to smooth manifolds, differential forms, De Rham cohomology, and the De Rham isomorphism.

Description
---
The **Directed Reading Program** (DRP) is a program in which undergraduate students are paired with graduate student mentors for
semester-long independent study projects. The mentee gives a 20-minute presentation on the topics covered at the end of the semester.
